# Duoseo – AI-Powered SEO & Digital Marketing Concept

Welcome! This repository explores **Duoseo**, a conceptual framework for AI-driven SEO, digital marketing, and automation strategies.

---

## What is Duoseo?

**Duoseo** represents the synergy of:

- **Duo** → Partnership, dual insights, collaborative intelligence  
- **SEO** → Search engine optimization, discoverability, and visibility  

Together, it symbolizes a next-generation approach to AI-assisted digital marketing and search strategies.

- Automated keyword research & analysis  
- Search visibility strategy for SaaS & tech products  
- Marketing automation and AI insights dashboards
